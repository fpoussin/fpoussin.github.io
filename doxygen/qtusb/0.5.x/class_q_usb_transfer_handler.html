◆ TransferStatus

enum QUsbTransferHandler::TransferStatus

Basically a copy of libusb's transfer enum.

Enumerator
transferCompleted 

Transfer completed without error. Note that this does not indicate that the entire amount of requested data was transferred.

transferError 

Transfer failed

transferTimeout 

Transfer timed out

transferCanceled 

Transfer was cancelled

transferStall 

For bulk/interrupt endpoints: halt condition detected (endpoint stalled). For control endpoints: control request not supported.

transferNoDevice 

Device was disconnected

transferOverflow 

Device sent more data than requested
